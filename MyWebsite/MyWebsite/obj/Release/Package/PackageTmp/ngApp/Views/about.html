The History

Mixed Up was created in early 2016 by a Full-Stack .NET developer named Duran Gradwell. To fulfill a dream of his own, he created this site as a dedicated community for new and experienced sound engineers, producers and musicians to review, advise and compare mixes with eachother and discuss related topics.
