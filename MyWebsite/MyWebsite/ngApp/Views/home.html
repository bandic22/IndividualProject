Welcome to the home recording platform!

The Community

Are you a musician taking up an interest in recording? A veteran engineer wanting to lend a helping hand to those starting out, or just show off? We know that the best way to improve your mixes is to get a second, or third opinion. Once you listen to the same mix 50 times, it becomes hard to notice some small details. Join our community, upload entire mixes, snippets, single instrument tracks and customize your profile with your list of gear, details of your space, and you'll have a community of recording-enthusiasts ready to give you their informed opinions and assistance.
